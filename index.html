Signature Events

Nurturing Talent Beyond Academics

We believe in holistic development, and our signature events provide a vibrant platform for students and parents to shine, fostering creativity, leadership, and community spirit.

Marya (आवाज SKC चा)

A platform exclusively for parents to showcase their talents & to re-create school / college memories, enhancing their hidden wisdom & intelligence.

Volcano

An opportunity to apply scientific processes, articulate research on a topic & express thoughts, enhancing communication skills.

Dance SKCian Dance

An event challenging students with different themes at each level, thereby elevating their dancing & leadership skills until they approach perfection.

Carnival

A fun-filled event to rekindle bonds & lip-smacking stalls put up by students which strongly enhance their business management skills.

SKCian's ART EXPO

A dream come true event for budding artists. An opportunity to display paintings in an ART gallery. Proceeds of sale are used to contribute for the education of Adivasi kids.

Expressionz

A golden opportunity to bring out innate capabilities in different contests. An event which kindles fire in students and inspires them to become achievers.
